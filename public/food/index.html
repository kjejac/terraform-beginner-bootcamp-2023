Pad Kana Moo Krob


Authentic Pad Kana Moo Krob
(Chinese Broccoli With Crispy Pork)

Ingredients

  • Pork belly
  • Chinese broccoli
  • Dried chilies
  • Oyster sauce
  • Golden mountain sauce
  • White sugar
  • Tamarind sauce
  • Garlic
  • Water

Instructions

Once you have fried the pork belly, the cooking process should take no longer than 5–10 minutes.
  1. Step 1: For frying the meat to crispy perfection, follow this recipe for how to make crispy pork belly. After frying, cut the meat in thin slices.
  2. Step 2:Mix all sauces (tamarind sauce, golden mountain, and oyster sauce) and white sugar + water in a bowl.
  3. Step 3:Heat oil in a large wok or skillet over medium heat. Add dried chilies and garlic, stir-fry for 20 seconds or until garlic turns golden.
  4. Step 4:Add the sauce mix from step 2, and let it simmer until the flavors are well combined.
  5. Step 5:Toss in the Chinese broccoli and cook until the leaves are wilted, but the stems are still slightly crisp.
  6. Step 6:Add the fried pork belly slices, and stir-fry briefly until they are heated through. Serve immediately.

How to serve

Serve with steamed rice, such as jasmine rice. Place a serving of rice on a plate and add a generous portion of the stir-fry to it.
